As you may understand, running a classroom requires rules that must be followed by students. I have simplified the main points here. But remember it is impossible to list all rules. Common sense should always prevail when making a decision about whether or not to do something.

  • Materials

  • Bring at least one usable pencil
  • Bring paper for taking notes
  • BRING YOUR BOOK (There is no excuse for not bringing it)
  • Bring your homework from previous nights. We have surprise homework quizzes and you not having your homework is not an excuse.

  • 59-0: F
  • Calculus: 12 Point Scale.
  • 100-88: A

  • 75-64: C
