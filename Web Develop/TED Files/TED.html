Tiger Electronic Display

TED is a touchscreen interactive computer system designed by EHS students over several years and represents the start of a program that will be expanded by future EHS students.
Please EXPLORE...but be nice...currently TED is:
  • sensitive to a single touch
  • enjoys math & maps
  • and likes fish when bored!
